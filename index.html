Bloodshed

Dark souls meets hunger games. On the board

Try to survive in the dead lands of the "Bloodshed" game, inhabited by wild animals, traps and daredevils like you. "Bloodshed" is an dark fantasy, intense PvP adventure and fighting board game.

Bloodshed is a turn-based adventure game where you would explore the castle and surroundings. The mysterious Castle will continuously change the landscape of the game, creating new challenges and opportunities to create tension between players.
Your goal is to find gold and treasures and escape alive, OR be the last one standing.
